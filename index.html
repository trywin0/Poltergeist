Poltergeist

Poltergeist is a sandbox game currently in development by me & other people I work with (freelancers & friends)
In Poltergeist you play, as well, a Poltergeist & you're able to freely roam around the map, which at the moment the only map is a suburban neighborhood.
You're able to flicker lights, break lights, open / close & slam doors, turn on sinks, flush toilets, open drawers, throw stuff around rooms, knock on walls, break windows & possess people.
Everything costs energy to do, the only way to get energy is to either succ it from electronics and/or scare people and feed off of their fear.
If you possess someone you're able to freely roam around in 1st person as them & use knives, guns, & even cars.
Once you've broken the law police will obviously come.
You can fight back but once you die or run out of energy you leave that body & return into poltergeist mode.
There are "Children of God" & "Children of Satan" - Children of Satan are automatically possessable whereas Children of God must be broken down, breaking them down means destroying their mental stability by scaring them, like nearly killing them.
There are priests so once the priests hear word of a haunting in a house they will come and purify that house, making the house not enterable anymore & making the people in that house not possessable.
